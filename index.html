MarketMental

A web app built on the MEAN stack, incorporating Material design and D3 visualization. MarketMental is designed to be a fun way to interact with and step into the world of the stock market. It uses data from the Twitter and Yahoo Finance APIs to allow users to discover the potential for correlations in the way the public sentiment views limelight companies and stock values and trends for the companies themselves. The user creates a fantasy portfolio and makes investments (with fake money) and is able to see the data of their success or failures over time courtesy of dynamic data display.

VYBZ

VYBZ is a mobile-centric app built on the MEAN stack and D3. The concept: Show me what's hot near me! Using embedded google maps, the app creates vibrant animated pulses of a size corresponding to the amount of users in a location, and of a color indicative of the VYBZ (or vibes) that users are inputing.

KidKarma

KidKarma is a Ruby on Rails web app that acts as a social network for kids. The traditional 'like' buttons are swapped out for a custom-built color-voting method that defines the karma of posts by a color scheme determined by the votes of the users themselves. Since most of the desired features are still in progress, the site in its current form simply offers blogging and voting.

sudoKuYo

A game designed for the few- Sudoku level: Samurai. A carousel with six Sudoku puzzles automatically rotates on a timer, forcing a user to switch thought focus and solve six puzzles at once. Built with Javascript and jQuery. I chose sudoKuYo as a game concept because of my interest in pursuing writing algorithms and my appreciation of the sudoku game logic. Future goal for this project would be to include a sudoku generator algorithm of my own creation.
